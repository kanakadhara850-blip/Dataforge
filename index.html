Who We Are

Not a club
that teaches.

DataForge is the technical data club at SRM IST Tiruchirappalli. We don't give certificates for showing up — we build operators who can clean, analyse, predict, and deploy end-to-end.

Everything we do is real work — real events, real datasets, real pressure. The Foundry Series is our proof.

Five domains. One mission. Culminating at YUVA 2026.

Learning Roadmap

Your path from
zero to operator.

Eight progressive milestones. Each one builds on the last.

01
Data Science & Statistics Fundamentals

Descriptive statistics, probability distributions, hypothesis testing, and the foundations of data-driven thinking. The bedrock everything else is built on.

02
Visualisation & Analytics Tool Mastery

Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly, Power BI, Streamlit. Build dashboards that reveal — not just display. Visual fluency as a core practitioner skill.

03
Real-World Decision Making

Apply analytics to business problems. Work with messy, incomplete, real-world data and extract decisions that actually matter under time and resource pressure.

04
Industry Frameworks & Large-Scale Systems

Big Data principles, ETL architecture, cloud pipelines, Spark basics. Understand how data moves and transforms at scale in real organisations.

05
Research

Read and critique papers. Reproduce results. Contribute to the R&D pipeline. Develop the capacity to go beyond tutorials into original analysis and experimentation.

06
Industry Connect

Direct access to professionals who judge and teach. DND Startup's learning track. Honest career roadmaps. Internship referrals based on your actual demonstrated skill.

07
Events & Datathons

The Foundry Series — 8 events, 4 chapters. Data Wars at YUVA 2026. Compete, build, and prove your skill on a live leaderboard in front of industry judges.

08
Real-World Impact

Deploy. Ship. Present. A portfolio of actual work — posters, dashboards, models, APIs — by September. Not certificates for showing up. Evidence of what you built.
